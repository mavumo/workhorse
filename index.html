Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers and solutions to queries, comments and questions commonly asked by our community.

Is Jobbr a recruitment agency?

+


JOBBR is not a recruitment or staffing agency, however, we do offer a comprehensive suite of tools and resources to connect job seekers and employers with little or no effort.

Can I sign up to find jobs as a freelancer?

+


Yes. We aim to provide our community with an all-in-one platform to find full-time, part-time, contract-based and remote work opportunities regardless of whether you're looking to be an employee or an independent contractor, sole proprietor or freelancer.

Do you offer recruitment or staffing services for employers?

+


Absolutely. We offer a turnkey solution for employers, recruiters and employment agencies looking to streamline, enhance or outsource their HR department and talent assessment/acquisition systems. Contact our support team at info@jobbr.co.za to set up a free 15-minute consultation today.

How long does it take to get hired on Jobbr?

+


While we cannot guarantee that you will find employment by using our services, we strive to ensure that you get the one-on-one, personalized service and support required to increase your chances of finding work significantly. Contact us via email or WhatsApp to find out more about our premium membership benefits for job seekers, freelancers and working professionals.

Why won’t my payment go through?

+


Our online payments are processed are powered by Payfast, a secure payment processing platform in South Africa. If you're having trouble making a payment on our platform, contact us via WhatsApp or send an email to info@jobbr.co.za and we'll be happy to assist.
